footprint How to do it

1

Find a quiet spot

Sit or stand quietly in your green space for 2 minutes before starting.

2

Observe the colors

Look for three different shades of green. Note where they appear.

3

The texture touch

Gently touch a leaf, a stone, and some bark. Describe the feeling.

visibility What to notice

"Observe how the child interacts with the smallest elements. Are they drawn to movement or stillness?"

  • • Changes in breathing or posture
  • • Duration of focus on a single object
  • • Vocalizations or shared pointing

history_toggle_off Why revisit later

Seasonal shifts change the garden entirely. By revisiting this template monthly, you create a beautiful record of environmental awareness and sensory development. It provides a consistent anchor for calm reflection.
